About

Hi, I'm Alice! I'm a third year Computational Media major at Georgia Tech. You can often find me tucked away in little nooks of Atlanta, chipping away at my latest side project. I love running at unbearably early moments of the day and creating in the dead of night. Art is thriving in Atlanta and I love witnessing it everyday in all of the forms it presents itself to be.

Outside of programming and design classes, I can be found participating in technical theatre. The culmination of sleepess nights, sawdust filled rooms, and meticulous cabling is an experience I would never trade. The feeling of creating a whole experience and story for an audience, working alongside people just as passionate as you, makes all the hard work worth it.

In general I am captivated and passionate about the love affair between tech and art and how it manifests itself in amazing ways. Being active in the technical and design part of theatre as well as creating in the world of digital media and tech has excited me. I feel most at home creating and thinking in all the different ways these different media and passions challenge me to do.
